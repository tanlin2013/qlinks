Z2 topological winding sector for triangular-lattice QDM/QLM on a torus.

The sector is the parity of occupied/electric-positive links crossing a non-contractible cut. The labels direction="a" and direction="b" refer to the two independent periodic cell directions of the triangular torus, not to filtering links by link.kind.
